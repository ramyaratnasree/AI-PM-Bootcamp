# AI PM Bootcamp

Hi! I'm **Ramya Ratna Sree Athukuri**.

After several years of building SaaS products as a Product Manager, I decided to deepen my expertise in AI by learning the best way I know - **building real products**.
This repository is my AI Product Management portfolio.

Instead of only reading books or taking courses, I'm documenting everything I learn while solving real customer problems.

## My Goals
* Learn modern AI technologies and workflows
* Become an AI Product Manager capable of building AI-native products
* Practice product discovery, customer research, prioritization, and execution
* Build a portfolio of real AI products from idea to launch
* Share my learning journey
